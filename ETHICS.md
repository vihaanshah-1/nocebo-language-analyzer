# Ethics & Responsible Use

This project is an educational NLP prototype exploring how language in
patient-facing medical text may influence patient expectations and anxiety
(nocebo-risk language).

## Not medical advice
This tool does not provide medical guidance, diagnosis, or clinical
recommendations. It should not be used to make healthcare decisions.

## Appropriate use
- Education and research prototyping
- Exploring language patterns in health communication
- Demonstration of applied NLP methods

## Inappropriate use
- Editing or approving clinical consent documents without professional review
- Using outputs as medical decision support
- Applying results to individual patient care

## Model limitations
- Small, manually labeled dataset
- Heuristic risk categories
- Language varies across institutions and patient populations
- Outputs may contain errors or oversimplifications

## Privacy
Do not paste any patient-identifying or private medical information into the tool.
